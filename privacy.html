Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Language Paisa. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information that may be processed through our educational platform. This policy outlines how we handle data.

Currently, Language Paisa operates as an open educational tool. We do not require user registration, and we do not collect personal identification information (such as names, addresses, or phone numbers) unless you explicitly contact us.

3. Local Storage and Cookies

To improve your experience, our website may use "Local Storage." This technology allows us to save your lesson progress directly on your own device. This data is not transmitted to our servers and remains under your control.

Our website utilizes standard industry services to function correctly, including Google Fonts and Tailwind CSS. These services may process technical data, such as your IP address, to deliver fonts and styles to your browser.

5. Data Security

We implement professional-grade security measures to protect the integrity of our platform. However, since we do not store personal data on our servers, the risk to your personal identification information is minimal.

Language Paisa reserves the right to update this policy as new features (like user accounts) are added. We encourage users to check this page periodically.
