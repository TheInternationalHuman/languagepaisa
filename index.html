Why pronunciation is so difficult

Many English learners understand grammar and vocabulary, but still feel nervous when they speak.

  • People ask them to repeat words
  • Pronunciation rules feel unclear
  • Some English sounds do not exist in their language
  • Online videos are often fast and confusing

This creates frustration and lowers confidence.
